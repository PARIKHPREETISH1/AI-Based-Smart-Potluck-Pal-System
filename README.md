## Overview
Potluck Pal is an end-to-end product concept designed to simplify meal coordination, grocery planning, and cost sharing for potluck-style gatherings.

Built as part of a New Product Development course, the product addresses common coordination challenges faced by frequent hosts and participants.

## Problem
Potluck planning today is fragmented across group chats, spreadsheets, and payment apps, leading to:
- Duplicate dishes and dietary conflicts
- Inefficient shopping and missing ingredients
- Awkward cost-splitting conversations

## Solution
Potluck Pal consolidates the entire workflow into a single experience:
- Group creation with shareable invites and QR codes
- Menu planning with allergen and dietary flags
- Smart, consolidated grocery lists
- Seamless cost tracking and Venmo integration
- Physical QR recipe cards linking dishes to digital profiles
